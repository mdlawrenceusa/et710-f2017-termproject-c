The Weeknd Bailed on VMAs Because He Just Wasn't Into It

Abel left a lot of people high and dry ... the MTV folks announced he'd perform. He was also nominated for four awards. Here's how it was put to TMZ ... The Weeknd's been on the road for a prolonged period of time and just didn't feel up to his VMAs gig. In his mind, he booked the gig a while ago, before concert fatigue set in, and he just felt differently in the days before the show. It's the second Weeknd event that got torpedoed in as many days.
