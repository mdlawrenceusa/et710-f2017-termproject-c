How We Started


While bored on deployment, and after about 30 days out to sea we grabbed a camera to make a "Music Video" that became a hit onboard the ship to
the rest of the crew. After our service in the United States Navy we decided to pursue our degrees in Media and Film. In January 2015 (approx 6
months after being discharged) we met at a bar in Michigan and decided to lay ground work on making a production company.
